Job Description

Job Title: Network Engineer

Location: UK Wide - Travel to Barrow

Description:

- Capgemini Cloud Infrastructure Services has an established global Cloud Practice, tasked with client advisory services, cloud project engagements and cloud service delivery. As a result of consistently high demand, the Cloud Practice is looking to increase the scale of the architect team by recruiting an experienced Network architect.

- The applicant will be capable of architecting, designing and implementing robust network solutions. The applicant will be a good team player with experience of working in geographically dispersed support environments and with third party network service providers.

- A highly technical role with a service and customer focus involving the design, evaluation and technical review of major network installations, implementation activities, client support, projects, transitions, due diligence exercises, and the development of new business solutions.

Your Role:

Capgemini is seeking to recruit a Network Engineer, ideally with experience of working in a large client outsourcing service environment, to work within a client facing project team. The applicant will be capable of designing and implementing robust network solutions. The applicant will be a good team player with experience of working in geographically dispersed support environments and with third party network service providers.

A good working knowledge of IP, Ethernet, routing and switching within LAN/WAN/Data Centre/ Public Cloud environments is essential. This includes:

- Software Defined Networks such as Cisco ACI, APIC ACI, MSO (Multi Site Orchestrator), NAE (Network Assurance Engine), ACI Anywhere; SD WAN e.g. Cisco vManage; SD Access e.g. DNAC, APIC EM, Cisco ISE.

- Automation/Orchestration: Network stack integration, Terraform, Ansible, Python, JSON, APIs.

- Security products e.g. Firewalls: Cisco (+Firepower Threat Defense), FortiGate, Fortinet SD-WAN solution.

- VMware NSX and other NFV products.

- Tooling e.g. Cisco Tetration, Solarwinds, CA

Your Profile:

- Proven experience of supporting complex network technical solutions

- Proven experience of implementing change within Data Centre LAN environments

- Practical working knowledge of WAN/LAN/ management/virtualisation technologies and products from leading vendors

- Experience of tooling for systems management

- Recognised Cisco or other industry certification

- The candidate will need to be able to gain security clearance for government work; this process can take place once they have joined Capgemini but any job offer will be dependent on SC clearance being obtained.
